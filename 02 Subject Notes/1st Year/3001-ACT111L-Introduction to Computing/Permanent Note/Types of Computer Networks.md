# Idea: 💡 Network Types Overview

✍ Summary:
- **LAN** (Local Area Network): Within a home, school, or office
- **MAN** (Metropolitan Area Network): Covers a city
- **WAN** (Wide Area Network): Covers global distances (e.g., the Internet)
