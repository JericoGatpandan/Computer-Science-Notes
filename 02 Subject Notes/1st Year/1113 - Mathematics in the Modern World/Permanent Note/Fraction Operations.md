# Idea: Fraction Operations

✍ Summary:
- **Add/Subtract**:
  - Similar denominators → Add numerators
  - Dissimilar → LCD, convert, then add
- **Multiply**:
  - Multiply numerators and denominators directly
- **Divide**:
  - Multiply by reciprocal of the second
