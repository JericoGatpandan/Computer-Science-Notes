# Idea: Ratio, Rate, and Proportion

✍ Summary:
- **Ratio**: Compares same units (e.g., 3:2)
- **Rate**: Compares different units (e.g., km/hr)
- **Proportion**: Equation with two equal ratios
  - **Direct**: Increase → Increase
  - **Inverse**: Increase → Decrease
