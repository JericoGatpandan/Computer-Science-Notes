# Idea: Translating Math Phrases

✍ Summary:
- Expressions: Like phrases (e.g., “2x + 6”) — incomplete thought
- Sentences: Have complete meaning with comparison (e.g., “2x + 6 > 10”)
- Example conversions:
  - "Six more than twice N" → 2N + 6
  - "N decreased by 5" → N - 5
