# Idea: 💡 ENIAC - Mauchly and Eckert

✍ Summary:
- **John Mauchly** and **J. Presper Eckert** created the **ENIAC** (1946), the first electronic general-purpose computer.
	It marked the beginning of the **electronic computing era**.
